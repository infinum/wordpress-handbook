Every team has its own culture and practices. Here are some of the practices that we follow in the WordPress team.

- Follow all the coding standards and practices.
- Contribute to the team's knowledge base.
- Contribute to the team's documentation
- Contribute to the team's codebase as this is a shared responsibility and you tool for everyday work.
- Follow the boyscout rule and leave the code better than you found it.
- Don't be afraid to ask for help.
- Help your colleagues and team members.
- Be proactive and take ownership of your work.
- Be open to feedback and criticism.
- Be open to new ideas and suggestions.
- Be open to new technologies and tools.
- Be open to new ways of working.
- Be open to new ways of doing things.
- Be open to new ways of thinking.
- Be open to new ways of doing things.
- Read, learn and grow.
- Be a team player.

By following these practices, you will be able to contribute to the team's success and grow as a developer. Also make sure to read the [Infinum Development Processes Handbook](https://infinum.com/handbook/devproc) and [Code of Conduct](https://infinum.com/handbook/general/essentials/infinum-values/code-of-conduct) documentation.
